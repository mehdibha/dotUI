### Summary

We collect the minimum data needed to run dotUI (account details, product telemetry, and optional analytics). We do not sell personal data. You control your data and can request deletion or export.

### What we collect

- Account: name, email, auth identifiers.
- Product data: event logs, settings, usage to improve stability and UX.
- Optional analytics/cookies where consented in EEA/UK.

### How we use data

To provide, secure, and improve the Service; communicate with you; and comply with law. We may use aggregated/anon data for analytics.

### Sharing

We share data with service providers (hosting, payments, analytics, support) under contracts. We do not sell personal data.

### Retention

We keep data only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law, then delete or anonymize it.

### Your rights

You can access, correct, export, or delete your data subject to legal exceptions. EEA/UK users have GDPR rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability, objection, restriction). Contact us via the website.

### International transfers

Where data moves internationally, we use safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

### Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, but no system is perfectly secure.

### Children

Our Service is not for children under 13 (or 16 where applicable). We do not knowingly collect their data.

### Cookies & tracking

See our Cookie Policy for details. In EEA/UK we ask for consent and honor choices.

### Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated in product or by updating the "Last updated" date.

### Contact

Questions or requests: use the contact details on our website.
